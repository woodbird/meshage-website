# Download & Install

Meshage supports multiple platforms. Choose the option that fits your device.

## System requirements

| Platform | Minimum version |
|----------|-----------------|
| **iOS** | iOS 15.0 or later |
| **Android** | Android 8.0 (API 26) or later |
| **Web** | Chrome 90+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+ or other modern browsers |

:::tip Quick try
**Fastest way**: Open the web app in your browser — no app download. Visit [meshage.io](https://meshage.io).
:::

## iOS (App Store)

1. Open **App Store**
2. Search for **Meshage**
3. Tap **Get** or **Download**
4. Open Meshage from your home screen

## Android

### Google Play (recommended)

1. Open **Google Play Store**
2. Search for **Meshage**
3. Tap **Install**
4. Open Meshage from your app list

### APK

If Google Play is unavailable, download the APK from the official site:

1. Go to [meshage.io/download](https://meshage.io/download)
2. Download **Android APK**
3. Open the APK and follow the prompts
4. If prompted about "unknown sources", allow installation from that source in Settings

:::warning Security
Only download the app from official Meshage channels. Third-party installers may compromise your account or data.
:::

## Web

1. Visit [meshage.io](https://meshage.io) in a modern browser
2. Tap **Get started** or **Web login**
3. Log in with an existing account or complete registration
4. Start using Meshage

Web feature set matches the app; suitable for desktop use and quick trials.

## After first launch

- **App**: Welcome or login screen; register or log in as prompted
- **Web**: Login/register page; you can stay logged in after creating an account

If you see install failures, crashes, or pages not loading, check your network or see [FAQ](/en/faq/).
