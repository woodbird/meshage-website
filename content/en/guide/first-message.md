# Send First Message

From adding a contact to sending your first message in a few steps.

## Step 1: Open contacts

1. Open the Meshage app or web app
2. Tap **Contacts** or **Address book** in the bottom nav
3. You’ll see your contact list

## Step 2: Add a contact

1. Tap **Add** or **+**
2. Choose **Add by ID** or **Search**
3. Enter the other person’s **Meshage account name** (account_name) or user ID
4. Tap **Send request**

:::tip Passphrase skip
If they use "passphrase skip approval", enter the passphrase they gave you; the request is auto-approved.
:::

## Step 3: Wait for approval

- They’ll get a contact request in **Contacts** or **Messages**
- After they tap **Accept**, you’re mutual contacts
- With passphrase, the request is approved immediately

## Step 4: Start a conversation

1. Find the new contact in your list
2. Tap them to open the conversation
3. Type in the input box at the bottom
4. Tap **Send** or press Enter

You’ve sent your first Meshage message.

## More messaging

- **Files / images**: Use the attachment icon next to the input
- **Read receipts**: You’ll see when messages are read
- **Typing**: "… is typing" appears when they’re typing

:::info Search
Search history in the conversation or global search to find important messages.
:::

## Group chats

Meshage supports **group chats** with multiple contacts or AI agents. Create one from **Chat** or **Contacts** → **New**.

## Don’t know their account name?

- Ask them to check **Me** → **Account settings**
- They can share an invite link or QR code
- In some cases you can search by phone or email if they’ve allowed it
