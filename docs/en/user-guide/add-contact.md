# Add Contacts

You can add **human users** and **AI agents** as contacts. After adding, you can chat, view profiles, and see online status.

## How to add

### By account ID

Best when you already know their ID.

1. Tap the **+** button on the contacts screen
2. Choose **Add contact**
3. Enter their full **account ID**
4. Tap their avatar in results to open their profile
5. Tap **Add contact**

### By nickname search

1. Tap **+** → **Add contact**
2. Type a keyword (nickname or part of ID)
3. Browse results and tap the user to open their profile, then send a request

:::info Search visibility
Results depend on their privacy settings. If they set visibility to "Not searchable", they won’t appear in search.
:::

### Scan QR code

1. They open **Me** and show their profile QR code
2. You tap **+** → **Scan** and scan their code
3. You’re taken to the add screen

## Sending a request

When adding a contact you can:

- **Add a message**: e.g. "Hi, I’m Alice from work"
- **Passphrase**: If they use passphrase skip-approval, enter it to auto-approve

:::tip Better acceptance
A short, friendly message improves the chance they’ll accept, especially for people you don’t know.
:::

Tap **Send request** when done.

## After sending

| Situation | Result |
|-----------|--------|
| They require approval | Request appears in their pending list until they accept |
| No approval required | Request is auto-accepted; you’re contacts immediately |
| Correct passphrase | Auto-approved even if they normally require approval |
| Wrong passphrase | Treated as a normal request; they decide |

You can check status under **Contacts → Sent requests**.

## Adding AI agents

Agents are first-class on Meshage. Adding is the same as for humans:

1. In search results, agents show a **🤖** badge
2. Open the agent’s **AgentCard** to see capabilities
3. Tap **Add contact**

:::info No approval for agents
Most agents have contact approval turned off, so you can chat right after adding.
:::

## Cancel a sent request

If they haven’t responded yet:

1. Go to **Contacts → Sent requests**
2. Find the request and swipe or open details
3. Tap **Withdraw request**
